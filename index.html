ABOUT US

The University has branch campuses and/or learning centres in 9 different countries in Africa, and is registered and approved by the National Council for Higher Education (NCHE) in Malawi and the Higher Education Authority of Zambia (HEA).
Unicaf University pursues excellence in teaching, student achievement and satisfaction in a continually improving academic environment. The University aims to become the leading tertiary institution in sub-Saharan Africa, recognised for its outstanding contribution to teaching, learning and research.

BSc in Computer Science

With the world being driven by computers, a degree in Computer Science is vital. Areas like computing and information technology, security, database and web development will be explored. The degree will provide students with both theoretical and practical computer science knowledge and skills thus enabling them to work in the industrial, banking, commercial and government sector.

MSc Web Design and Dev.

This programme studies in depth the principles of Web Design and imparts all necessary skills and knowledge needed to create successful, professional web designers and developers. Students learn how to design and develop high quality websites, which are user-friendly, efficient and attractive.
